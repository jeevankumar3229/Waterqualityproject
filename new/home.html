Measurements for Safe Water - Do’s & Don’ts

DO'S

Reuse of water whenever possible


Use Sprinkler for irrigation


Use scientific method of application fertilizers


Fix leaks properly


Install low-flow shower heads


DON'T

Don't keep tap running while having bath, brushing teeth, washing dishes


Avoid throw dead bodies in a river


Don't wash clothes and kitchen utensils in water bodies

Never dump anything to the water bodies


Avoid use of pesticides
